# Sample Prompts

These prompts were used to manually test NutriLink during the proof-of-concept period.

## Resource Lookup Prompts

- Where can I find food assistance in Hinds County?
- What food resources are available near Jackson, Mississippi?
- What organizations help with food insecurity in Hinds County?
- Where can someone apply for SNAP benefits?
- What local resources can help a family that needs groceries?
- Are there community organizations that help with food access?

## Nutrition Guidance Prompts

- What is HER/SWAP guidance?
- What foods are considered healthier under HER/SWAP?
- What does the green, yellow, and red food ranking mean?
- I ate chicken, rice, soda, and chips. Is this healthy?
- I ate a bag of chips today. Is this okay?
- I want to eat healthier foods. What do you suggest?

## County Context Prompts

- Why was Hinds County selected for this PoC?
- What food insecurity issues affect Hinds County?
- How can county-level health data help with food recommendations?
- What kinds of health indicators were considered?

## Project Explanation Prompts

- What is NutriLink?
- What data sources does NutriLink use?
- How does NutriLink use Amazon Bedrock?
- What are the limitations of this PoC?
- What future work could improve NutriLink?

## Demo Prompts

- Help me find food resources in Hinds County.
- I want healthy food options and nearby assistance.
- Explain how HER/SWAP helps classify foods.
