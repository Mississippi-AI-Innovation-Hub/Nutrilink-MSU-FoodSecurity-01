Hinds County, Mississippi

Purpose:
Hinds County is the pilot county for the NutriLink proof of concept.

County context:
- Food insecurity is a concern.
- Nutrition-related chronic disease risk, including diabetes, is a concern.
- Food assistance resources may include SNAP/MDHS, Mississippi Food Network, local food pantries, and faith-based/community food programs.

POC note:
This system gives general nutrition-security guidance and is not medical advice.
